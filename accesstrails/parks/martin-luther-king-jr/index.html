Martin Luther King, Jr. Park

Accessibility rating: Partially Accessible

Near SPARC Alexandria

Accessibility Ratings

Each amenity was scored against an ADA-based checklist. N/A means the amenity is absent; parks are not penalized for it. See the methodology for how scores were assigned.

Parking

6/6

There are multiple accessible parking spaces

Trails

N/A

This park does not have any trails, it has a swimming pool and tennis courts.

Bathrooms

7/7

There is a fully accessible bathroom available in the pool area, which has limited hours and is not open during the winter.

Visitors Center/Clubhouse

N/A

There is no visitor's center. There is a pool house but it is only available to employees.

Picnic Shelter & Seating Areas

4/5
  • Nice picnic area with one wheelchair accessible table.
  • There is an accessible grill.
  • The trash can is not accessible, however there is an accessible recycling bin near the tennis courts close by.

Signage

3/4

There are not very many signs, but they do not contain Braille and may be somewhat far away from an accessible path.
