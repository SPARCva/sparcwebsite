Potomac Crossing Park

Accessibility rating: Mostly Accessible

Near SPARC Leesburg

Unique Feature: Accessible Playground

This park has playground features accessible for wheelchairs.

Accessibility Ratings

Each amenity was scored against an ADA-based checklist. N/A means the amenity is absent; parks are not penalized for it. See the methodology for how scores were assigned.

Parking

6/6

There is one fully accessible paved parking space in a gravel parking lot.

Trails

2/7

There is a very short section of flat, gravel trail that runs alongside the park, but as soon as it gets to the woods it becomes rough and inaccessible to wheelchairs.

Bathrooms

7/7

There is a clean, large, accessible porta potty on site near the shelter.

Visitors Center/Clubhouse

N/A

There is no visitors center at this park.

Picnic Shelter & Seating Areas

5/5
  • There is a fully accessible shelter beside the parking lot with an accessible table in a well placed location.
  • There is an accessible grill at the picnic structure.
  • There are accessible trash cans and pet cleanup bags.

Signage

5/6
  • There is a good, wheelchair height, sign at the start of the paved section of the trail.
  • The sign includes slope and cross slope percentages.
