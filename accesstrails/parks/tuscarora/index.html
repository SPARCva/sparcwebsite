Tuscarora Park

Partially Accessible

Near SPARC Leesburg

Accessibility Ratings

Each amenity was scored against an ADA-based checklist. N/A means the amenity is absent; parks are not penalized for it. See the methodology for how scores were assigned.

Parking

6/6
  • The accessible parking spaces are marked with the international accessibility symbol.
  • There is adequate maneuverability around the accessible parking.

Trails

3/7
  • The trail gets slightly steep from the playground down to the main trail, but is manageable.
  • After about a quarter mile it turns into a bike trail with two lanes so bikes can maneuver around a wheelchair.
  • There are no signs indicating how to get to the main trailhead.

Bathrooms

7/7
  • There is an accessible bathroom at the main picnic structure.
  • There is also an accessible porta potty in the parking lot.

Visitors Center/Clubhouse

N/A

There is no visitors center at this park.

Picnic Shelter & Seating Areas

3/5
  • There is an accessible table in the shelter, however it was up against a pillar which would make it difficult to maneuver to.
  • The grills and trash cans are accessible.

Signage

2/6

There are not many signs, and there are no signs down to the trailhead which is not immediately visible from the parking lot.

Specially Adapted Resource Centers provides community-integrated day programs for adults with severe and multiple disabilities in Northern Virginia. We believe every adult deserves a meaningful life filled with connection, purpose, and belonging.
