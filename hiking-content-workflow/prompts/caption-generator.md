# Hiking Content Caption Generator — Claude Prompt Template

Copy and paste this prompt into Claude, filling in the bracketed sections with
your hike details. Claude will generate platform-optimized captions, hashtags,
and posting recommendations.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a social media content strategist specializing in outdoor/hiking content
for TikTok and Instagram Reels. Generate optimized captions and hashtags for my
hiking content.

## Trail Information

- Trail Name: [TRAIL NAME]
- Location: [CITY, STATE]
- AllTrails Rating: [X.X / 5] (or "Not on AllTrails")
- Difficulty: [Easy / Moderate / Hard / Expert]
- Distance: [X.X miles]
- Elevation Gain: [X,XXX ft]
- Date Hiked: [DATE]
- Key Features: [sunset, waterfall, scramble, views, wildflowers, etc.]
- Season/Weather: [sunny, snowy, fall colors, etc.]
- Personal Experience Note: [one sentence about what made this hike special to you]

## Content Details

- Platform: [TikTok / Instagram Reel / Both]
- Video Style: [photo slideshow / video highlights / mix]
- Audio Used: [song name or "original audio"]
- Target Audience: [beginner hikers / experienced hikers / general outdoor lovers / local community]
- Account Niche: [adventure / wellness / local exploration / family hiking / solo hiking]

## Generate the Following

### 1. Instagram Reel Caption
- Strong hook (first line that makes people stop scrolling)
- 2-3 sentences about the trail experience
- Trail stats line (📍 location, ⭐ rating, difficulty)
- Call-to-action (ask a question, prompt saves/shares)
- 3-5 hashtags (mix of broad + niche + location-specific)
- Alt text for accessibility

### 2. TikTok Caption
- Short, punchy hook (under 150 characters including hashtags)
- 3-5 hashtags optimized for TikTok discovery
- Note: TikTok captions should be shorter and more casual than Instagram

### 3. Instagram Story Text
- Short overlay text for reposting the Reel to Stories
- Poll or question sticker suggestion to boost engagement

### 4. Hashtag Strategy Notes
- Explain why you chose each hashtag
- Suggest 3 alternative hashtag sets for A/B testing across future posts

### 5. Best Posting Times
- Recommend specific day/time for each platform
- Consider the hiking/outdoor audience behavior patterns

## Rules
- Do NOT use #fyp, #foryou, #viral, or other generic spam hashtags
- Keep hashtag count to 3-5 per platform (quality over quantity)
- Write captions that sound natural, not like a brand or AI
- Include at least one location-specific hashtag
- If the trail is NOT on AllTrails, suggest a relevant nature/hiking quote
  instead of rating stats
- Make the hook create curiosity or emotion, not just describe the content
```
