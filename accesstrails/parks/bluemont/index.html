Bluemont Park

Accessibility rating: Mostly Inaccessible

Near SPARC Arlington

Unique Feature: Accessible Baseball Field

This park has an accessible baseball field and viewing area.

Accessibility Ratings

Each amenity was scored against an ADA-based checklist. N/A means the amenity is absent; parks are not penalized for it. See the methodology for how scores were assigned.

Parking

6/6

There is plenty of accessible parking near the main shelter.

Trails

5/7
  • There are long, fairly flat walking and biking trails.
  • The trail from the parking lot to the East trail system has one narrow area that is only 4 feet wide.
  • The path from the parking lot to the West trail system is wide and easily accessible.

Bathrooms

0/7
  • There are no accessible bathrooms.
  • The bathrooms are marked with the international sign of accessibility, however there is a step to enter the bathroom and no maneuverability space, and no accessible features in the bathroom.

Visitors Center/Clubhouse

N/A
  • There is no visitors center at this park.
  • There is a park service building at the main shelter with a small office only accessible to park staff.

Picnic Shelter & Seating Areas

5/5
  • There are two picnic shelters, which are both accessible.
  • The main shelter is nice and spacious, and the path to the shelter is wide enough for a wheelchair, however watch for some overgrown bushes over the path.
  • The second shelter is down the West path, and has an accessible table.

Signage

3/6
  • The whole area has both new and old signs, the newer signs have high contrast color and text, but the old signs don't.
  • None of the signs contain Braille, and some are higher than wheelchair height and could be hard to see.
