About the Event

Join us for the ribbon cutting ceremony at the SPARC Alexandria Center. Your generosity and commitment to SPARC's mission have made something truly extraordinary possible, and we warmly invite you to celebrate this milestone with us.

The ceremony will highlight the Exploration Sensory Lab -- a one-of-a-kind mobile environment designed to transform the lives of the adults with disabilities we serve across Northern Virginia. This fully mobile, controlled multisensory environment is engineered to meet a wide range of sensory needs -- providing calming regulation for participants who become overstimulated, and active engagement opportunities for those who seek stimulation. Because it travels across all five of SPARC's centers and accompanies participants into the community, this lab dramatically expands our capacity to deliver individualized care wherever it is needed most.
