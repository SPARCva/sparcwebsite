♿ Plan your accessible visit

The essentials for getting in the door, in one place. Fuller details are in the sections below.

🚪 Accessible entrance
The main entrance on 21st Street NW is wheelchair accessible, with a second accessible route near the H Street NW corner. Volunteers are stationed at both.
♿ Accessible parking & drop-off
GW’s ADA-compliant garages ($24/day) are the closest option — the Student Center Garage is directly across H Street from the venue. Prefer not to self-park? GW Hospital valet (flat $25/day) lets you hand the car off at the door.
🚇 Step-free Metro route
Foggy Bottom–GWU (Blue / Orange / Silver) is elevator-equipped and about 0.2 miles from the venue over step-free sidewalks with curb cuts. Check elevator status before you travel.
🚻 Restrooms
On the first floor (off the lobby) and the lower level, both reachable by elevator.
🧡 Sensory space
SPARC’s MEL sensory van is parked by the main entrance all day — ask any volunteer.
🤙 ASL & captioning
An ASL interpreter and live captioning will be provided throughout the program.

Area Map: Parking, Metro & Food

The nearest Metro station is Foggy Bottom–GWU (Blue, Orange, and Silver lines). The station is elevator-equipped and about 0.2 miles from the venue, along step-free city sidewalks with curb cuts. If you are driving, GW’s visitor garages are ADA-compliant and cost $24 for the day — they accept credit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Wallet only (no cash). Street parking around the venue is metered with a 2-hour limit on weekdays, so a garage is a better choice for the full-day program. DC’s red-top meters are reserved for vehicles displaying a disability placard or tags.

If you would like a guaranteed space, two commercial garages a block from the venue can be reserved in advance through ParkMobile or ParkWhiz — use the reserve links on the map or in the parking list below.

Metro

Foggy Bottom–GWU station (Blue, Orange, Silver) — entrance at 23rd & I (Eye) St NW, about 0.2 miles from the venue: exit the station, head east on I St, then turn right onto 21st St; the MPA building is at the corner of 21st & H. The station has an elevator to street level — check WMATA’s Foggy Bottom station page for real-time elevator status.

Food nearby

  • Western Market (food hall) — 2000 Pennsylvania Ave NW, 0.1 miles away. 11 quick-service vendors; step-free entry and accessible seating.
  • Founding Farmers DC — 1924 Pennsylvania Ave NW, 0.2 miles away. Popular sit-down American; reservations strongly recommended.
  • Tonic at Quigley’s — 2036 G St NW, 0.2 miles away. Casual American comfort food.
  • Circa at Foggy Bottom — 2221 I (Eye) St NW, 0.2 miles away. American bistro with a large patio, on the way to/from the Metro.
  • Duke’s Grocery — 2000 Pennsylvania Ave NW, 0.1 miles away. East-London-style pub famous for its Proper Burger; sit-down, takes reservations.
  • Bindaas Bowls & Rolls — 2000 Pennsylvania Ave NW, 0.1 miles away. Counter-service Indian street food — bowls, kati rolls, chaat.
  • District Commons — 2200 Pennsylvania Ave NW, 0.1 miles away. Modern American tavern, roomier sit-down option for groups.
  • Sweetgreen — 2221 I (Eye) St NW, 0.1 miles away. Salads and warm bowls, counter service.
  • GW Delicatessen — 2133 G St NW, 0.2 miles away. Neighborhood deli; breakfast all day, sandwiches, smoothies.
  • Carvings — 2021 F St NW, 0.3 miles away. Deli and coffee bar; hearty sandwiches, counter service.
Good to know: complimentary refreshments and coffee will be served on the second floor of the venue during breaks, so you only need to leave the building if you’d like a full lunch.

Venue Floor Plans

Text version of floor plans

First floor (street level)

Enter from 21st Street NW through the main entrance, which is wheelchair accessible; SPARC’s MEL sensory van is parked just outside it. The registration and check-in table is immediately inside, followed by the lobby, where SPARC volunteers will be stationed to help. Doors from the lobby lead into the Jack Morton Auditorium (fixed seating, open seating with wheelchair spaces; the stage is at the front). Restrooms, the elevator, and stairs are all located off the lobby on this level. A second accessible route into the building is available near the H Street NW corner.

Second floor (Art Gallery Lobby)

The second floor holds the Summit Posters & Exhibits displays and the Refreshments & Coffee catering tables, with café tables and standing tables (plus chairs) for breaks. Reach it by the elevators or the stairs from the first-floor lobby.

Lower level

The lower level has additional restrooms. Reach it by the elevator or the stairs down from the first-floor lobby.

Floor plans are schematic and provided for event wayfinding. Layout confirmed with GW event staff; report corrections to erica@sparcsolutions.org.
