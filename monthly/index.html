Why Monthly Matters

SPARC is the only nonprofit in Virginia providing community-integrated day programs for adults with severe and multiple disabilities who require 1:1 support. Monthly gifts give us something rare and essential: predictability.

Steady & Sustainable

Recurring gifts let us plan programming, staffing, and scholarships months in advance — instead of scrambling between grant cycles.

Direct to Participants

Every dollar fuels art, music, life skills, sensory tools, community outings, and the 2/3 scholarship every SPARC family automatically receives.

Belonging, on Repeat

Your monthly gift advances the ADA integration mandate — turning legal promise into lived reality for adults who would otherwise have nowhere to go.

Where Your Monthly Gift Goes

SPARC's work directly advances the community integration mandate established by the ADA and enforced through the Supreme Court's Olmstead v. L.C. decision and the U.S. v. Virginia Olmstead settlement. Your sustained giving helps Northern Virginia fulfill its legal and moral commitments to equity — while ensuring that every SPARC family, regardless of income, has access to person-centered programming five days a week.

Monthly Giving Questions

Is my monthly gift tax-deductible?

Yes. SPARC is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (EIN: 20-5513060). You'll receive an annual tax receipt summarizing your monthly giving for the year.

Can I change or cancel my monthly gift?

Absolutely. Every Stripe receipt email includes a secure link to manage your subscription — you can update your payment method, change your amount, or cancel at any time. You can also reach Erica Gaffney at erica@sparcsolutions.org or (703) 817-5839 for any help.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes. All monthly gifts are processed by Stripe, a PCI‑DSS Level 1 certified payment provider trusted by millions of businesses worldwide. SPARC never sees or stores your card details.

How is my gift used?

Monthly gifts directly fund daily programming at SPARC's five Northern Virginia centers — including art, music therapy, life skills classes, adaptive movement, sensory supports, community outings, and the 2/3 tuition scholarship every SPARC family automatically receives.

What if I'd prefer to give a one-time gift?

One-time gifts are equally appreciated and equally impactful. Visit our main donation page to give a single gift of any amount.
