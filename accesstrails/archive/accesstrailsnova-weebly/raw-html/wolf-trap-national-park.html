Wolf Trap National Park
6/6
PARKING
There is plenty of accessible parking near the trailhead, but you have to drive past the event parking spaces and through the employee parking to get there. 

The accessible parking near the trailhead is not available on days when there are concerts unless you arrive in the morning. 
5/7
TRAILS
The trail on the left toward the gazebo is partially paved and mostly flat. 

The trail to the right is rougher and would be more difficult to maneuver in a wheelchair. 
6/7
BATHROOMS
There is a family accessible bathroom with hand rails and maneuverable space. 

The soap and hand dryer are out of reach for wheelchair height. 
5/5
VISITORS CENTER/CLUBHOUSE
The concert venue is accessible from venue parking with a clear path to accessible seating areas. 
4/5
PICNIC SHELTER/ SEATING AREAS
There are multiple, well placed accessible tables in the seating area. 

The seating area is uncovered. 

The amphitheater in the woods at the end of the trail also has accessible seating areas. 

The trash cans are out of reach. 
3/6
SIGNAGE
The signage is near the main amphitheater and there are no directional signs that guide you to it. 

​There is no Braille.
