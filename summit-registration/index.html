Advancing Dignity for Adults with Severe and Multiple Disabilities

Register below

About the Summit

Adults with severe and multiple disabilities experience some of the highest levels of social isolation, limited access to meaningful community engagement, and families living with constant financial, emotional, and caregiving strain.

The SPARC/GW Summit brings together individuals with lived experience, families, advocates, researchers, policymakers, and community partners for a one-day working forum focused on solutions and collective action.
